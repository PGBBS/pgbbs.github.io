Welcome to this collection of bits and pieces by some (not so random) Passau Graduate Students. They are technically built from a github-repository using Jekyll. And they are philosophically founded as a [do-ocracy](https://communitywiki.org/wiki/DoOcracy): if you think something should be on these pages, put it here. If you see something that could be improved, do it. (The whole thing is version-controlled.) On the one hand, there are (very) rough notes on topics that we discuss while simultaneously enjoying a meal (see "Posts" below). On the other hand, there's general information that we refer to (and are asked for) regularly (see "Pages" below).
